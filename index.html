About me

Hello!
I started my educational journey at Escola Secundária de Castelo de Paiva, opting for supplementary education. During this period, I not only completed my science course, but I also immersed myself in the world of music by studying at the Castelo de Paiva Music Academy for six years.

In 2021, I entered the Instituto Superior de Engenharia do Porto (ISEP), where I am currently in the 3rd year of my Computer Engineering degree. In October 2023, I embarked on the Erasmus+ adventure, which led me to study for a semester at Politechnika Łódzka, in ŁÓDŹ, Poland. This Erasmus experience not only shaped my academic path, but also enriched my personal life, providing unforgettable memories and building a solid foundation for my personal and professional growth.
